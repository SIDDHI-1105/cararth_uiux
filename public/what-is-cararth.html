Why we exist

India's used car market is broken. Buyers struggle to find honest listings buried under paid promotions. Small dealers can't compete with big players who buy top spots. Individual sellers are invisible unless they pay for "featured" placement.

Cararth refuses to participate in this pay-to-win game. We're building a trust-first marketplace where visibility is earned through data quality, not budget. Our AI verification technology ensures every listing meets authenticity standards before appearing in search results.

Our mission

To create the most trusted layer for used‑car discovery in India where search results are fair, unbiased, and genuinely helpful to buyers and sellers alike.

Our vision

A marketplace where buyers find the most relevant car without worrying about who paid for visibility—and sellers compete on quality, not marketing budgets.

How we build trust

Transparency requires technology. Our verification system combines multiple AI models and data sources to ensure listing authenticity:

  • Aggregator & canonicalizer: We normalize listings from multiple sources into a standard format, making comparisons effortless.
  • Listing health engine: Every listing is scored for completeness, image quality, pricing consistency, and freshness.
  • AI verification suite: Image authenticity checks, duplicate detection, RTO cross‑verification, and natural‑language consistency analysis.
  • Trust scoring: Verification signals combine into visible trust badges and fair ranking signals.
  • Local relevance: City-first architecture ensures you see cars actually available in your area.

Why our business model matters

Most car marketplaces make money from paid listings, creating a fundamental conflict: they earn more when sellers pay for visibility, not when buyers find the right car. This model incentivizes promoting listings that generate revenue over listings that match buyer needs.

Cararth's revenue comes from optional value-added services that help sellers without distorting search results: verification badges, enhanced analytics, and lead quality tools. We'll never sell ranking positions.

Our success depends on user trust, not advertiser budgets. When you find the right car faster, we've done our job.
