      <h1>What is Cararth? — India’s No‑Paid‑Listings Used‑Car Search Engine</h1>
      <p class="lede">Cararth is a trust‑first used‑car search engine launching in Hyderabad with a simple but radical promise: <strong>no paid listings</strong>. This page explains Cararth’s mission, values, technology, roadmap and how it intends to reshape used‑car discovery across India.</p>
      <p class="small">Published: <span id="pub-date">16 Nov 2025</span> • Canonical: <a href="https://www.cararth.com/what-is-cararth">https://www.cararth.com/what-is-cararth</a></p>
    </header>

    <div class="grid">
      <main>
        <section class="card">
          <h2>Quick answer</h2>
          <p>Cararth is India’s first <strong>no‑paid‑listings</strong> used‑car search engine that ranks cars by relevance, freshness and verification rather than advertising spend. It launches in Hyderabad and will scale PAN‑India with a focus on transparency and fairness.</p>

          <h2>Key takeaways</h2>
          <ul>
            <li><strong>No paid listings:</strong> Visibility is earned through data quality, not budget.</li>
            <li><strong>Mission driven:</strong> Build a fair and transparent used‑car discovery layer for India.</li>
            <li><strong>Tech + Trust:</strong> AI‑assisted verification and standardised listings improve decision making.</li>
            <li><strong>Seller parity:</strong> Owners and small dealers get equal visibility without ad spend.</li>
          </ul>
        </section>

        <section class="card">
          <h2>Cararth’s mission, vision and promise</h2>
          <h3>Mission — Transparency first</h3>
          <p>To create the most trusted layer for used‑car discovery in India where search results are fair, unbiased and genuinely helpful to buyers and sellers alike.</p>

          <h3>Vision — A fair marketplace ecosystem</h3>
          <p>To enable every buyer to find the most relevant car without having to pay attention to who paid for visibility — and to enable sellers to compete on quality, not marketing budgets.</p>

          <h3>Our promise to users</h3>
          <ul>
            <li>Zero paid boosts: no one can buy first place.</li>
            <li>Transparent listing data: standardized, comparable and honest.</li>
            <li>Trust by design: AI verification and clear seller signals.</li>
          </ul>
        </section>

        <section class="card">
          <h2>What Cararth stands for — Core values</h2>
          <ul>
            <li><strong>Transparency over promotion</strong> — every decision is data‑driven and auditable.</li>
            <li><strong>Information over influence</strong> — we prioritise accurate information over marketing influence.</li>
            <li><strong>Fair discovery</strong> — sellers compete on product quality, not ad spend.</li>
            <li><strong>User-first ethics</strong> — product decisions are made to protect buyer interests.</li>
            <li><strong>Open citation network</strong> — strong GEO and dealer citations to help local discovery.</li>
          </ul>
        </section>

        <section class="card">
          <h2>Value proposition — Functional, emotional & economic value</h2>
          <h3>Functional</h3>
          <ul>
            <li>Standardised listings for quick comparison</li>
            <li>AI verification and trust badges</li>
            <li>City‑first architecture for GEO relevance</li>
          </ul>
          <h3>Emotional</h3>
          <ul>
            <li>Peace of mind when shortlisting</li>
            <li>Relief from manipulative promoted listings</li>
          </ul>
          <h3>Economic</h3>
          <ul>
            <li>No listing fees for basic posts</li>
            <li>Lower acquisition costs for small dealers</li>
            <li>Improved price discovery for buyers</li>
          </ul>
        </section>

        <section class="card">
          <h2>Ethical stance — Why we refuse paid ranking</h2>
          <p>Paid ranking creates a conflict: it makes visibility a function of budget, not relevance. This harms buyers and forces dealers into pay‑to‑win cycles. Cararth refuses to participate — we prioritise long‑term user trust over short‑term revenue. Our commercial model focuses on optional verification services, lead quality tools, and value‑added seller features that do not alter ranking.</p>
        </section>

        <section class="card">
          <h2>Technology framework — How Cararth builds trust</h2>
          <p>Without exposing proprietary IP, here’s the high‑level tech stack and components that power Cararth’s fairness and verification:</p>
          <ul>
            <li><strong>Aggregator & canonicaliser:</strong> Normalises incoming listings from dealers, marketplaces and owner feeds into a canonical car model/variant taxonomy.</li>
            <li><strong>Listing health engine:</strong> Scores completeness, image quality, pricing parity and freshness.</li>
            <li><strong>AI verification suite:</strong> Image authenticity, duplicate detection, RTO cross‑checks and natural‑language consistency checks.</li>
            <li><strong>Trust scoring:</strong> Combines verification signals into a visible trust badge and ranking boost within fair rules.</li>
            <li><strong>GEO & entity graph:</strong> City → Dealer → Brand → Model linking for strong local signals and internal citations (SOP compliant).</li>
          </ul>
        </section>

        <section class="card">
          <h2>Roadmap — How Cararth will scale</h2>
          <p>Cararth’s phased plan balances product maturity and geographic reach:</p>
          <ul>
            <li><strong>Phase 1 (Hyderabad launch):</strong> Core search, AI verification, dealer onboarding and city pages.</li>
            <li><strong>Phase 2 (3–6 months):</strong> PAN‑India city rollouts, brand‑in‑city pages, dealer activation workflows.</li>
            <li><strong>Phase 3 (6–12 months):</strong> API for partners, standardised listing export/import, reputation system for dealers.</li>
            <li><strong>Phase 4 (12+ months):</strong> Advanced features — EV battery health indicators, lease/finance integrations, negotiation assistant.</li>
          </ul>
        </section>

        <section class="card">
          <h2>How we measure success</h2>
          <ul>
            <li>Time to first meaningful contact (higher = better UX)</li>
            <li>Listing Health score improvements across feed partners</li>
            <li>Reduction in disputed listings post‑verification</li>
            <li>Dealer acquisition cost for small dealers</li>
            <li>City page organic rankings and citation growth (per SOP)</li>
          </ul>
        </section>
