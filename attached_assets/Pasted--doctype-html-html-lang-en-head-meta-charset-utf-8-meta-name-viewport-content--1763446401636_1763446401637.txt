    <article>
      <h2>Why Cararth exists — the problem with pay‑to‑rank used‑car sites</h2>
      <p>Most used‑car portals let dealers or sellers pay to promote their listings. This pay‑to‑rank model often surfaces inventory that’s paid to be visible, not necessarily the best option for the buyer. The result: noisy search results, hidden bargains and high acquisition costs for dealers.</p>

      <h2>How Cararth works — three pillars of a fair search</h2>

      <h3>1. No paid listings — ever</h3>
      <p>Cararth does not offer paid boosts, featured slots or promoted placements. Visibility is earned through listing completeness, fresh updates, image quality, accurate pricing and seller verification. This levels the playing field for private owners and small dealers.</p>

      <h3>2. AI‑assisted verification</h3>
      <p>Cararth applies AI checks (image authenticity, RTO‑style data cross‑checks, text consistency) to flag suspicious or low‑quality listings. Verified listings receive a trust badge and rank higher in relevant searches.</p>

      <h3>3. Standardised listing format</h3>
      <p>Every listing follows a consistent template: variant, year, km, fuel, transmission, owner count, service history indicator, price benchmark and seller type. This reduces decision friction and improves comparison.</p>

      <h2>Why Hyderabad is the launch city</h2>
      <p>Hyderabad offers a strong mix of organised dealers, tech‑savvy buyers and a growing certified pre‑owned ecosystem, making it an ideal market to pilot Cararth’s no‑paid‑listings model before a PAN‑India roll‑out.</p>

      <h2>How Cararth helps buyers and sellers</h2>
      <h3>For buyers</h3>
      <ul>
        <li>Unbiased results that prioritise relevance and verification</li>
        <li>Cleaner price discovery with less noise from promoted listings</li>
        <li>Ability to compare similarly‑scoped listings easily</li>
      </ul>

      <h3>For sellers</h3>
      <ul>
        <li>Equal opportunity for visibility without ad spends</li>
        <li>Lower customer acquisition cost for smaller dealers</li>
        <li>Access to tools for verification and better presentation (premium optional services)</li>
      </ul>
