Functional Requirements:  
1. **Frontend Design:**  
   - Use collapsible accordion-style FAQ boxes (e.g., each question expands on click).  
   - Clean, minimal, responsive design — match CarArth’s theme colors (#0b3a66, white, teal accents).

2. **Content (Preload FAQs):**  
   - **Q1:** What is CarArth.com?  
     **A:** CarArth.com is India’s first AI-powered used car search engine for both buyers and sellers, bringing listings from multiple sources into one smart, unified experience.  

   - **Q2:** How does CarArth find cars from multiple platforms?  
     **A:** CarArth uses legally compliant web crawlers (Firecrawl and Apify) to fetch publicly available listings and organize them for easier discovery.  

   - **Q3:** How is CarArth different from Cars24 or OLX Autos?  
     **A:** CarArth is a search engine, not a dealer. It aggregates verified listings from different platforms instead of selling directly.  

   - **Q4:** Is CarArth data legal and verified?  
     **A:** Yes. CarArth only indexes publicly available information and complies with Indian data and intermediary laws.  

   - **Q5:** How can sellers list their cars?  
     **A:** Sellers can list via CarArth’s syndication form and have their car appear on multiple platforms simultaneously.  

   - **Q6:** What are the benefits of using AI in CarArth?  
     **A:** AI helps match buyers to the most relevant listings, ensures zero duplication, and provides price insights using data from SIAM and market trends.  

   - **Q7:** Does CarArth charge sellers or buyers?  
     **A:** Basic listing is free; premium syndication and featured listings are optional paid upgrades.  

   - **Q8:** Where does CarArth operate?  
     **A:** The pilot phase is live in Telangana, with expansion plans across India.
