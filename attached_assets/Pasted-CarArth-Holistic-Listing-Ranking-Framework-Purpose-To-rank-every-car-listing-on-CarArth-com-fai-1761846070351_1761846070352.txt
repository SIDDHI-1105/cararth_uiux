ğŸ§® Listing Score Formula
Weighted Components
Factor	Weight	Description
Pricing Fairness	30%	How close listing price is to average market price
Recency	15%	Freshness of listing (age decay after 30 days)
Demand	20%	Market demand for that model/variant
Completeness	15%	% of required fields and images filled
Image Quality	10%	Visual clarity and number of photos
Seller Trust	10%	Verified AI / Dealer / User type
Sub-score Computation
1. Price Score
price_score = max(0, 1 - |list_price - avg_price| / avg_price) * 100
2. Recency Score
recency_score = max(0, 1 - days_since_listing / 30) * 100
3. Demand Score
demand_score = demandIndex * 100
4. Completeness Score
= percentage of required fields completed Ã— 100
5. Image Quality Score
image_score = ((min(image_count, 5)/5)*0.4 + imageQualityAvg*0.6) * 100
6. Seller Trust Score
Seller Type	Trust Value	Score
CarArthX Ethical AI	0.95	95
CarArthX Exclusive Dealer	0.90	90
CarArthX User (verified)	0.85	85
Unverified User	0.60	60
Final Score Calculation
listingScore =
  (price_score * 0.30) +
  (recency_score * 0.15) +
  (demand_score * 0.20) +
  (completeness_score * 0.15) +
  (image_score * 0.10) +
  (seller_trust_score * 0.10)
